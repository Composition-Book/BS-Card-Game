BS Game Steps

Step-by-Step Breakdown

1. Game Setup

  • Create the Deck: Generate and shuffle a 52-card deck.
  • Distribute Cards: Divide the deck among players, accounting for uneven splits.
  • Set Player Order: Decide play order and initialize turn tracking.
  • Initialize Game State: Create a discard pile and track the current rank and players' hands.

2. Gameplay Loop

Player Turn: Claim a play, add cards to discard pile, and call "BS" when needed.

2.1 Player's Turn

  • Claim a Play: Select and declare cards to play.
  • Add Cards to Discard Pile: Update the discard pile and player hand.

2.2 "Call BS" Check

  • Call Decision: Players decide to challenge the claim.
  • Resolve BS: Verify the claim and assign the discard pile to the appropriate player.

2.3 Update Turn State

  • Advance Turn: Update the current rank and move to the next player.
  • Check for Winner: End the game if a player empties their hand.

3. Computer Player Logic

  • Decide Cards to Play: Bluff or play matching cards based on strategy.
  • Call BS Logic: Evaluate the likelihood of a false claim and decide whether to challenge.

4. Endgame

  • Declare Winner: Announce the first player to empty their hand.
  • Restart or Exit: Offer an option to replay or quit.

Example Gameplay

Start: Player 1 claims to play "1 Ace." Discard pile updated.

Challenge: Player 2 calls BS, claim revealed false. Player 1 takes the pile.

Next Turn: Move to Player 2 and update rank to "2."
